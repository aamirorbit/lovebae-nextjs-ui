## Tips for Flirty Texting

### Timing Matters
- Good morning texts: 7-9 AM
- Midday check-ins: Around lunch
- Spicy texts: Evening hours only!
- Goodnight texts: Before they typically sleep

### Read the Room
- Match their energy
- Don't send spicy texts if they're in a meeting
- If they're stressed, prioritize supportive over flirty

### Keep It Fresh
- Don't use the same line every day
- Reference recent events or inside jokes
- Mix up sweet, flirty, and spicy

### Be Authentic
- Use your own voice
- Adapt these templates to sound like you
- The best texts feel genuine, not scripted
