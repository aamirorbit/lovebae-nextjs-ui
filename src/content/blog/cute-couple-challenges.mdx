Social media has given us some of the most entertaining (and relationship-testing!) couple challenges. Whether you want to post them online or just have fun together, these challenges will have you laughing, bonding, and maybe questioning how well you really know each other.

## Classic Challenges

### 1. The Whisper Challenge

**How it works:**
- One person wears noise-canceling headphones with loud music
- The other whispers a phrase
- The headphone wearer tries to lip-read

**Why it's fun:** The misinterpretations are hilarious, and you'll get inside jokes for days.

### 2. The Blindfolded Makeup Challenge

**How it works:**
- One person is blindfolded
- They apply makeup to their partner

**Why it's fun:** The results are always chaotic and memorable. Pro tip: Use cheap makeup!

### 3. The Not My Arms Challenge

**How it works:**
- One person stands behind the other, arms through their partner's shirt
- The person in back controls the "arms" for eating, makeup, or tasks

**Why it's fun:** It requires incredible teamwork (and usually fails hilariously).

### 4. The Guess That Song Challenge

**How it works:**
- Play the first second of a song
- Partner has to guess the title and artist

**Why it's fun:** Use your shared playlist for extra nostalgia points.

### 5. The Accent Challenge

**How it works:**
- Each person attempts different accents
- Partner guesses which accent you're trying to do

**Why it's fun:** Bad accents are somehow even funnier than good ones.

## "How Well Do You Know Me" Challenges

### 6. The Girlfriend/Boyfriend Tag

**How it works:**
- One person asks questions about the other
- They have to answer without help

**Questions to include:**
- When's my birthday?
- What's my biggest fear?
- What's my dream vacation?
- What's my go-to coffee order?
- What makes me happiest?

### 7. The Finish My Sentence Challenge

**How it works:**
- One person starts a sentence
- Partner finishes it

**Examples:**
- "My favorite thing about you is..."
- "You always make me laugh when..."
- "I knew I loved you when..."

### 8. The Psychic Challenge

**How it works:**
- Both write down answers to questions separately
- See how many match!

**Questions:**
- What's our next vacation destination?
- What will we eat for dinner tomorrow?
- What movie will we watch next?
- What's my current mood?

### 9. The Assumption Challenge

**How it works:**
- Share assumptions you have about each other
- Partner confirms or denies

**Example assumptions:**
- "I assume you cried watching The Notebook"
- "I assume you've stalked my social media before we dated"
- "I assume you talk about me to your friends"

### 10. The Password Challenge

**How it works:**
- Try to guess your partner's phone password, Netflix PIN, etc.
- Reveal what it actually is

**Why it's fun:** You learn what numbers are meaningful to them (hopefully your birthday?).

## Physical Challenges

### 11. The Trust Fall Challenge

**How it works:**
- One person falls backward
- Partner catches them

**Level up:** Do it from increasing heights (safely!) or with eyes closed.

### 12. The Couples Yoga Challenge

**How it works:**
- Attempt partner yoga poses together
- See how long you can hold them

**Start with:**
- Partner tree pose
- Seated twist together
- Flying (where one person balances on the other's feet)

### 13. The Handcuffed Challenge

**How it works:**
- Handcuff (or tape) yourselves together for a set time
- Try to complete normal tasks

**Tasks to try:**
- Make a meal
- Get dressed
- Clean the house

### 14. The Couples Workout Challenge

**How it works:**
- Create a partner workout routine
- Complete it together without complaining

**Include:**
- Partner planks
- Synchronized squats
- Wheelbarrow walks

### 15. The No Thumbs Challenge

**How it works:**
- Tape your thumbs down
- Complete everyday tasks

**Tasks:**
- Open a jar
- Tie shoes
- Text a message
- Eat a meal

## Relationship-Testing Challenges

### 16. The Bean Boozled Challenge

**How it works:**
- Spin the wheel on Bean Boozled jelly beans
- Eat whatever you land on (could be delicious or disgusting!)

**Why it's fun:** Watching your partner eat a "barf" flavored jellybean is entertainment gold.

### 17. The Extreme Sour Challenge

**How it works:**
- Eat increasingly sour candies
- First to tap out loses

**Try:** Warheads, Toxic Waste, or lemon juice shots.

### 18. The Spicy Food Challenge

**How it works:**
- Eat progressively hotter hot sauces
- See who can handle the most heat

**Safety note:** Have milk and bread ready!

### 19. The Don't Laugh Challenge

**How it works:**
- Take turns trying to make each other laugh
- First to crack loses

**Tactics allowed:**
- Funny faces
- Jokes
- Tickling (if agreed upon!)

### 20. The Silent Treatment Challenge

**How it works:**
- Spend a set time communicating only through writing or gestures
- No speaking allowed

**Why it's fun:** You'll appreciate verbal communication so much more afterward!

## Romantic Challenges

### 21. The 7 Second Challenge (Compliment Edition)

**How it works:**
- You have 7 seconds to give a genuine compliment
- Take turns until someone can't think of one

**Why it's fun:** You'll hear things your partner has never said out loud.

### 22. The Love Letter Challenge

**How it works:**
- Set a timer for 5 minutes
- Both write a love letter
- Read them to each other

**Why it's fun:** Even if writing isn't your thing, the effort is meaningful.

### 23. The "How We Met" Retelling Challenge

**How it works:**
- Each person tells the story of how you met
- See how different (or similar!) your versions are

**Why it's fun:** You'll discover details you never knew about those early days.

### 24. The Recreate Our First Date Challenge

**How it works:**
- Plan a date that mirrors your very first date
- Try to remember and recreate every detail

**Why it's fun:** Pure nostalgia and a reminder of why you fell for each other.

### 25. The 100 Days of Connection Challenge

**How it works:**
- Commit to 100 days of intentional connection
- Use Lovebae to track daily check-ins, love notes, and shared moments

**Why it's fun:** Building a streak creates accountability and deepens your bond.

## Challenge Tips

### Before You Start:
- Agree on rules and boundaries
- Make sure both partners are comfortable
- Have supplies ready
- Clear your schedule to fully enjoy it

### During the Challenge:
- Don't take it too seriously
- Laugh at yourselves
- Be a good sport if you lose
- Focus on fun, not winning

### After the Challenge:
- Share the video if you recorded it
- Talk about what you learned
- Plan your next challenge
- Use Lovebae to document your memories

## Why Challenges Strengthen Relationships

Couple challenges aren't just social media content — they actually help your relationship by:

- **Creating shared experiences** — Memories you'll reference for years
- **Encouraging teamwork** — Many challenges require cooperation
- **Building inside jokes** — "Remember when you guessed THAT accent?!"
- **Lowering defenses** — Being silly together builds intimacy
- **Adding novelty** — New experiences release bonding hormones
